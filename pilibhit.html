Pilibhit

            A tour of Pilibhit would offer a blend of wildlife exploration, cultural experiences, and historical visits. 
            Here are some highlights you might expect:

- **Pilibhit Tiger Reserve**: A safari adventure observing tigers in their natural habitat.
- **Chuka Beach**: A picturesque spot along the Sharda Sagar Dam, perfect for picnics and boat rides.
- **Gauri Shankar Temple**: An ancient temple with intricate architecture, reflecting the spiritual side of Pilibhit.
- **Raja Venu Ka Tila**: Archaeological site showcasing the remnants of ancient civilizations.
